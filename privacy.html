Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: we collect nothing. The extension has no server, no analytics, no tracking of any kind. Your conversations stay between you and the AI.

What we collect

Nothing. HidePrompt does not collect, store, transmit, or process any data about you or your usage.

How the extension works

The extension runs entirely in your browser. When you toggle the prompt bar, it applies or removes a CSS class on the page — that's it. No network requests are made. No content is read. No keystrokes are logged.

Third parties

We have no third-party integrations. No analytics service. No crash reporting. No advertising network. Nothing is shared with anyone because nothing is collected.

Open source

HidePrompt is fully open source. You can read every line of code on GitHub and verify these claims yourself. There is nothing hidden.

Contact

If you have any questions, open an issue on GitHub.
