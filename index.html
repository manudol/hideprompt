Features
01
One-click toggle
Click the button in the toolbar or use the keyboard shortcut. The prompt bar disappears instantly, giving you full vertical space to read.
02
Zero data collected
The extension reads nothing and sends nothing. It purely toggles a CSS class. Your conversations stay entirely between you and the AI.
03
Keyboard shortcut
Hit ⌘⇧L on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+L on Windows — prompt bar gone in under 200ms. No mouse required.
